TRIANGLE

A tablet and smartphone solution for private economy

Triangle is a tablet and smartphone application prototype designed to help managing personal budgets. The program consists of a vizualization based on data extracted from your daily receipts. The data is subsequently converted into large triangles each representing every dayâ€™s expenses. The sizes of the triangles are shaped after the amount that is spent that particular day. Within these larger triangles one hundred smaller triangles are placed each representing 1 percent of a days overall expenses. The smaller triangles will at the end of day be categorized in different colors according to the type of products that is purchased that day.
