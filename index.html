Acoustic Leak Detection

Winner of UXD Award 2023 in the Product Category - ux-design-awards.com

The jury said:
"This B2B solution can have a critical impact on water supply and management throughout the world. It has been developed together with users and customers and has a clear business model. The visual design is straightforward and fits the user group. The jury recognizes the significance of ‘Acoustic Water Leak Detector’ – this solution shows how service design and technology can scale up impact in the public domain."

The ultrasonic smart water meters hear the leaks that cannot be seen and the Leak Detector analytics software uses machine learning to process the noise and identify the specific location of the leaks in the pipes.

In EU alone, 23% of drinking water is lost in distribution networks. Only 10% of those leaks are visible above ground.

The solution helps water utilities identify leaks underground to minimize this water loss and save money and resources.

 

Three key take-aways for the project

1. Challenge from a UX Perspective

  • Problem: Initial product was time-consuming and complex for users to filter and analyze data for decision-making.
  • UX Goal:
    • Enable efficient data analysis to minimize time-on-task.
    • Highlight impactful actions (e.g., pinpointing major water leakage sources).
    • Ensure user confidence with a high success rate in task completion.

2. Personal Highlight in the Process

  • Approach:
    • Leveraged early prototyping using the dual-track model:
      • Created and tested simple A/B mockups before coding.
      • Validated solutions through user interviews, shadowing, and observations.
    • Iterated designs based on real-world feedback, ensuring effective problem-solving.
  • Insight:
    • Discovered diversity in customer and end-user needs across countries, emphasizing continuous collaboration with users and domain experts.

3. Vision for the Future

  • Long-Term Focus:
    • Address the global challenge of ensuring access to clean water through enhanced UX solutions.
    • Continuously adapt designs using user insights and data to meet evolving needs in a fast-changing digital environment.
    • Build agile, collaborative teams capable of rapid iteration to deliver impactful, user-centered solutions.
